What is a vegetarian?

A person who does not eat meat.

Why do people choose to become vegetarian?

There are many reasons a person might choose to become vegetarian. From religion to concern over animal rights and environmental health, to dietary preferences, the reason to go vegetarian varies from person to person.

What is a vegan?

A person who does not eat or use animal products.
