  7. What does gluten-free mean?

    8. For a food to be labeled as gluten-free, the FDA states that it must contain no more than 20 parts per million (ppm) of gluten. Source.

  8. Why are people gluten-free?

    9. People with celiac disease can suffer adverse effects from consuming gluten from products such as pasta. People also willingly choose to be gluten-free because they feel it is a healthier lifestyle. Source.
